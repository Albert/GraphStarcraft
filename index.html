Instructions:

  1. Add Buildings with the "+ Building ↓" button (in the top left)
  2. Add Tasks with the "+ Task →" button (next to each building)
  3. Click & drag to move tasks around horizontally
  4. Watch below as your resources graph adjusts accordingly (colors: Minerals, Gas, and supply: Current / Cap)
  5. To save, click on the "Chart → Text" button and copy the text somewhere safe
  6. To load your text, paste it in the textfield and click the "Text → Chart" button
